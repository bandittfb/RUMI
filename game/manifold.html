RUMI · COLLAPSE the manifold

a deduction puzzle on the surface of a spinning solid
Goal: every cell on the solid is secretly live or void. Deduce them all from the numbers and collapse the whole manifold.

The rule: a number = how many cells are live among itself + its 4 touching neighbours (the plus around it). A 0 → all 5 void. A 5 → all 5 live.

Play: drag to spin the solid · click a cell to mark it (Fill ◆ / Empty ◇ tools, or left-click = fill, right-click = empty). A correct mark cascades across the surface — and ripples around the back where you can't see. Solve it across all six faces.

Glowing cells have a guaranteed move. Hover a number to read its math. 💡 hint spins you to one.
