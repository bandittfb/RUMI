RUMI · COLLAPSE

a logic puzzle — solve the grid with the number clues
Goal: every square is secretly filled or empty. Work out every square using the numbers — solve the whole grid to win.

The only rule: a number tells you how many of the 9 squares around it — its own cell plus the 8 touching it — are filled.

0
a 0 → all 9 are empty
9
a 9 → all 9 are filled
4
a 4 → exactly 4 filled

How to play: click a square to mark it filled ◆ · right-click to mark it empty ◇ (or pick a tool below, then click). Get a square right and the numbers around it chain-react, auto-solving their neighbours. Get one wrong and you lose a little stability (top-right).

Stuck? A cyan-glowing number always has a guaranteed move — start there. Hover any number to see its math. Or press 💡 hint.
