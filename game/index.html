RUMI · COLLAPSE

a deduction game where your inference detonates

Every cell hides a state: live or void. A number is a clue — how many of the nine cells in its 3×3 block are live (the clue's own cell counts).

Deduce a cell, then collapse it. A correct collapse cascades — forcing its neighbours, which force theirs — a chain reaction of pure logic. The art is finding the keystone: the one cell whose collapse detonates the furthest.
